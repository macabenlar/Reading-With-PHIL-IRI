2. Data Collection and Use

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services. Below are the types of data we collect:

Personal Data

While using our app, we may ask you to provide personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. This includes:

  • Teachers: Name, email address, and account-related information.
  • Students (Grade 5 and Grade 6): Parent or guardian's email address, and usage data. We no longer collect students' email addresses directly to ensure that we have parental consent before processing any personal data for children.

Usage Data

Usage data is automatically collected when you use the app, including:

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and unique device identifiers.
  • For students, we may collect usage data about how the app is used, including time spent on activities, quizzes, and interaction with educational content.

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app’s services.
  • To manage your account and enable features available to you.
  • To communicate with you about updates, service notifications, and offers (if applicable).
  • To improve your experience with the app and tailor it to your educational needs.
  • For business transfers or legal compliance.

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Your Data

We may share your data in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: To help us run the app and for analytics purposes.
  • Business Transfers: If we undergo a merger or sale of assets, your data may be part of the transferred assets.
  • Affiliates: We may share your data with our affiliates to support the app’s functionality.
  • Other Users: When teachers assign tasks or share materials, this may be visible to students.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your data to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

4. Children’s Privacy (Grade 5 & Grade 6)

Our Service is intended for use by Grade 5 and Grade 6 students in the Philippines. We are committed to complying with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and ensuring the privacy of minors.

  • Parental Consent: We obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting personal data from children under 13. Personal data collected from students will include only the parent’s email address and necessary educational information.
  • Data Collection: Personal information collected from students includes their name and parent's email address for communication and educational purposes.
  • Rights of Parents: Parents or guardians can request access to, correction, or deletion of their child’s data by contacting us at macabenlar.d@gmail.com.

How We Collect Parental Consent

We will ask parents or guardians to provide consent via email or other secure methods. No personal data will be collected from students without this consent.
